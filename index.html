About me

I am a Web Development student from Phoenix Arizona, with experience in CSS, HTML and Javascript, hoping to one day transition into software more generally.

Ever since I recently drove through the streets of Seattle for a few hours while I was waiting for a flight, my short term career goal has been to complete my program, get a job in Seattle, and then move there permanently. I probably lived there in a past life, in the 80s or something.

Other than Web Development, my other interests include Film Directing, Film Writing, Film Producing, and Game Development.

Development experience

I program every day, between working on school assignments and personal side projects.

I look forward to meeting and collaborating with like minded people, with whom I can help build something worthwhile and polished. This is probably what I most look forward to in entering this industry, is the connections I'll form and the things they will allow me to do which I wouldn't be able to do alone.
